# Design DNA — Presage

## Visual Personality: dark-modern
## Layout: product-led
## Typography: bold-display

### Color Palette
- Primary (Deep Navy): #0D1B2A — `oklch(0.15 0.04 240)`
- Accent (Electric Amber): #F5A623 — `oklch(0.76 0.16 65)`
- Surface (Dark Navy Card): #111D2C — `oklch(0.17 0.04 240)`
- Text (Near-White): #E8EDF4 — `oklch(0.93 0.01 240)`
- Muted Text: `oklch(0.60 0.02 240)`

### Font Pairing
- Display: Archivo Black — weight 900 (massive hero headings, stat numbers)
- Body: Barlow — weights 300–600 (tight, data-forward, readable)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **"War Room" Hero**: Deep navy base with electric amber radial glow asymmetrically placed top-right. Grain/noise overlay for premium texture. Stats strip (markets, volume, creator earnings) floats just under the headline for above-the-fold social proof.
2. **Dramatic type scale contrast**: Hero headline at text-7xl–8xl via Archivo Black 900. Key numbers at text-5xl. Body copy Barlow 300–400 at 18px. The scale gap creates intelligence-platform tension: confident data, not a casino.
3. **Glowing amber CTA with ambient halo**: The primary button has a radial amber glow behind it — feels live, interactive, like a target on a mission briefing screen.
4. **Market card product mockup**: Styled HTML card showing a prediction market with probability bar, creator earnings badge, and volume — product context without needing a real screenshot.
5. **Data-line section dividers**: Thin 1px amber/20% horizontal rules echo Bloomberg Terminal aesthetic throughout — purposeful structure.
